K-Start

About

K-Start is a non-profit organization that specializes in helping the homeless turn their lives around. We offer many different programs that help teach professional and life skills, so they can begin kickstarting a better future for themselves.

Sponsors

Being a non profit organization, sponsors, volunteers and donations are very helpful for us, and the people that go through our program. By sponsoring us that will allow for better equipment and a better curriculum, to help boost the learning proccess.

Testimonies

We have many testimonies from the people that have succeded in kickstarting their new life. The testimonies include information on how the program affected the individual, and how much life has changed, from before they went through the program and where they are now.
