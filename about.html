About us

K-Start, which stands for Kick-Start was founded for the sole purpose of helping the homeless people seek a career. We specialize in helping them find a better job to live a better life. Giving a helping hand to someone in need is what we do and we have the best sponsers and connections for that.
Our first succesful launch was just this year in 2020 after being provided by the best networking help possible. We believe everyone deserves a second chance and can always learn if willing to be taught. Helping homeless people all across Charlotte.

What areas do we serve

We are a non-profit organization that is currently serving the loacal Charlotte-Mecklenburg area. While we continue to grow our plan is to expand outside of Charlotte and into other bigger cities!

Our Vision, Mission, and Values

K-Start is currently helping the local Charlotte-Mecklenburg area but we want to see that expand outside into other major cities in the next years to come. K-Start creates a networking system built around helping the homeless find a starting career in a life long memorable process. We continue to place anyone in any career possible as its never impossible if you keep trying. We Value teamwork, trust, hardwork, and time.
